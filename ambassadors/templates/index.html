{% block FAQ %}

FAQ

Not sure how payment works? Confused about how to get promotional materials? Good news — you’re in the right place. This is a list of the top questions from Ambassadors.

How do you know where to send my Ambassador Package?

When you applied to the ambassador program you were asked to provide your mailing address. If your mailing address has changed, or you think you made a mistake, please email us.

How do your track who has clicked/installed from my personal link?

Each ambassador is given a unique link to identify them. When someone uses that link on their iPhone/android they will be automatically sent to the Figure 1 page in the App Store. Here's where the fun starts. On my end, I'm able to see (1) each persons link (2) how many people clicked their link (3) how many people downloaded Figure 1 with their link.

I’ve completed a Milestone, when will I get my prize?

Prizes are sent out once a month.

How will you transfer me the money for the Milestone I completed?

We transfer the money for completed Milestones once a month via Visa gift card. If you would prefer not to receive a transfer via Visa gift card we can send you an Amazon or Starbucks gift card of equivalent value instead.

How can I get promotional materials?

Once you have completed Milestone 3 you can request promotional materials be sent to you by contacting us. We’re happy to send you business cards, information app cards, printed case books or work with you to develop printed materials.

Why can’t I see the Ambassador Badge on my profile?

You will only receive the Ambassador Badge on your profile once you reach Milestone 1. If you’ve reached Milestone 1 and it still isn’t showing up on your profile, please email us.

How do I request a letter of reference?

We can offer a letter of reference in PDF or written directly on your LinkedIn Profile once you’ve passed Milestone 3. Email us if you’d like us to write one. Feel free to specify anything you’d like us to mention in the letter.
